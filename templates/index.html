Recommended Learning Path

  1. Text Preprocessing - Clean and prepare raw text (tokenization, lowercasing, stopwords, punctuation)
  2. Part-of-Speech Tagging - Identify grammatical roles of words
  3. N-Gram Modeling - Explore local context with uni/bi/tri-grams
  4. Chunking & Parsing - Group words into phrases and visualize structure
  5. Named Entity Recognition - Extract people, places, organizations, and more
  6. Sentiment Analysis - Measure emotional tone and polarity
  7. Text Classification - Assign texts to categories using features/signals
  8. Word Embeddings - Represent words as dense vectors capturing meaning
  9. Machine Translation - Translate between languages with neural models
  10. Speech Processing - Convert speech to text (STT) and text to speech (TTS) in the browser
  11. Text Summarization - Condense long passages into concise summaries
  12. Text Generation - Generate coherent continuations from prompts
  13. Topic Modelling - Discover latent themes with LDA
  14. Question Answering - Extract precise answers to questions from a given context
